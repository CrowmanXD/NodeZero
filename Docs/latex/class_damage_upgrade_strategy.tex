\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Strategy for increasing the player\textquotesingle{}s damage output. 

This concrete strategy handles the logic for purchasing a damage increase. It interacts with the \doxylink{struct_game_config}{Game\+Config} to determine costs and increment values.

This strategy increases the damage dealt per tick to enemies within the player\textquotesingle{}s damage zone.
